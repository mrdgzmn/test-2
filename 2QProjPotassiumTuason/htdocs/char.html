CHARACTERS

YATORA YAGUCHI

Yatora Yaguchi is the protagonist of the manga Blue Period. When he discovers the beauty of art in his second year of high school, he decides that he wants to attend the Tokyo University of the Arts (Geidai).

YOTASUKE TAKAHASHI

Yotasuke Takahashi is a student Yaguchi meets at cram school. Despite being relatively new to the art they are studying, when he places his piece on the wall, Yaguchi is drawn in by the breathtaking composition.

RYUJI AYUKAWA

“Yuka” is a student from Yaguchi’s high school. They attended cram school with Yaguchi for a while before they quit. Their heart had never been in the art they specialized in – it was only what their family wanted for them.

HARUKA HASHIDA

Hashida met Yaguchi at cram school. Since then, they have gone to several museum trips together, where he taught Yaguchi about understanding art more. Hashida worked at a kid’s painting class with Yaguchi for a while.

MAKI KUWANA

Maki Kuwana attended the same cram school as Yaguchi. Both of her parents and her sister attended Geidai.

MARU MORI

Mori was a senior that attended the same high school as Yaguchi. She painted the F-100 that caused Yaguchi to fall in love with art.

MASAKO SAEKI

Masako Saeki is the moderator for the art club of Yaguchi’s high school. She becomes Yaguchi’s employer later in the series.

MAYU OOBA

Mayu Ooba is the instructor for Yaguchi’s cram school. She teaches him about art theory and she helps him develop his own style.

YAKUMO MURAI

Murai is a student from Geidai. He shows the class the artwork he believes is the most powerful he has ever created. He says he will never create anything more powerful than that.

KINEMI MIKI

Kinemi Miki is a student attending Geidai. On the day of the entrance exam, she broke Yaguchi’s mirror which was being used to paint self-portraits.

KENJI HACHIRO

Kenji is a student from Geidai. He is friends with Murai and Momoyo, and lets Murai stay over at some point in the manga.

KOIGAKUBO

Koigakubo is a student from Yaguchi's high school. He is inspired by Yaguchi's decision to attend art school, which leads to him enrolling in a pastry school.

UTASHIMA

Utashima is a student from Yaguchi's high school. He often goes drinking with Yaguchi and his other friends.

SUMIDA

Sumida is a student from Yaguchi's high school. He often goes drinking with Yaguchi and his other friends.

MARIA HANAKAGE

Maria Hanakage is an assistant instructor from Geidai. She was mistaken for a student during Yaguchi's first day at college.

SAKURAI

Sakurai is an assistant instructor from Geidai. She does not appear as much as her fellow assistant instructors.

YUMESAKI

Yumesaki is an assistant instructor from Geidai. He is seen accompanying the students to their field trips and giving them drinks during their festival preparation.

AMO NEKOYASHIKI

Nekoyashiki is an instructor from Geidai. Despite her bubbly personality, she gives harsh criticism, especially towards Yotasuke’s art.

ROSEI

Rosei is an instructor from Geidai. He does not appear as often as his fellow instructors.

TSUKINOKI

Tsukinoki is an instructor at Geidai. He is often criticizes Yaguchi’s work, causing Yaguchi to begin to doubt his abilities as an artist.

AYANO AIZAWA

Ayano is a student from Geidai. She is often seen with Miki, especially during the festival preparation.

MOMOYO KAKINOKIZAKA

Momoyo is a student from Geidai. She is friends with Murai and Kenji, and is mostly active during the festival preparation chapters.
