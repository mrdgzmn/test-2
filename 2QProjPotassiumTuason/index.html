BLUE PERIOD

synopsis

The story falls under the coming of age genre for the "seinen" demographic. It centers around Yatora Yaguchi, the "perfect" high school student with a good reputation and excellent grades. However, beneath the surface, he deals with the emptiness and frustration of having no ambition in his life.
One day, he comes across an artwork that would spark his love for painting. Now with a new purpose, he embarks on a journey to face all kinds of people and conflicts to prove himself in the world of art.

author

Tsubasa Yamaguchi is a Japanese manga artist, who graduated from Tokyo University of the Arts. She created two one-shots (Nude Model, and Onna no Ko) before she created a manga adaptation of the film "She and Her Cat" The manga ran for three months, and one year later, she started Blue Period.
